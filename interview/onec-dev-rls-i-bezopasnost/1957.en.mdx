# RLS with temp and virtual tables

## In short

RLS applies to real tables in the query, including sources of register virtual tables. `ПОМЕСТИТЬ` temp tables do not get RLS automatically: if a TT holds foreign references, filter when filling the TT.

## How to answer in an interview

- I build TT with filter for allowed companies in the same query.
- I do not copy a full catalog into TT and hope for later RLS.
- Register virtual tables inherit limits on the underlying register.
- Privileged query for TT bypasses RLS entirely.
- I am honest: joining TT to documents without fill-time filter leaks rows.

## How it works

```bsl
"ВЫБРАТЬ Док.Ссылка ПОМЕСТИТЬ ВТДок
|ИЗ Документ.Реализация КАК Док
|ГДЕ Док.Организация В (&ДоступныеОрганизации)"
```

The platform binds the parameter from the profile on user execution.

## Pitfalls

**TT from privileged query.** Full dataset to client path.
**TT without company filter.** Leak via later join.
**Expecting RLS on TT name.** Not applied.
**Mixed sessions.** Background job vs user report.
**Duplicate filter in TT and RLS.** Redundant but safer than a hole.
