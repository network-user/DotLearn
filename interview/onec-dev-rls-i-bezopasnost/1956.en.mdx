# RLS in distributed infobases and exchange

## In short

RLS applies in each infobase session context. Exchange moves data without user RLS; filtering must be in exchange rules and on receive. Nodes may differ in profiles and company sets.

## How to answer in an interview

- I do not expect central node RLS to auto-trim export.
- I configure filter by exchange node and company in rules.
- On edge nodes the same objects are seen by users with different profiles.
- Load runs in privileged exchange context without RLS.
- I am honest: "see in HQ, not in branch" is often different access groups, not exchange bug.

## How it works

`ОбменДанными.Загрузка = Истина` disables business logic and RLS on write. After load users read by local profiles.

## Pitfalls

**Export whole catalog.** Edge gets extra companies.
**Access groups not synced.** Same logins, different access.
**Exchange test as admin.** Miss RLS on receive.
**Manual edit after exchange.** Drift from central.
**RLS instead of node filter.** Wrong distribution architecture.
