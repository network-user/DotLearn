# Rules for writing fast queries in 1C

## In short

Narrow with parameters and virtual tables, avoid queries in loops, minimize fields and joins, use `В(&Список)` instead of long OR, materialize small filters with `ПОМЕСТИТЬ`.

## How to answer in an interview

- One query for a batch of references beats thousands in a loop.
- Register virtual tables with filters in parentheses.
- I do not compute in SELECT what can filter in WHERE.
- I check plans on a stand with volume close to production.
- I am honest: premature `ПОМЕСТИТЬ` on the whole register slows down, not speeds up.

## How it works

Batch read:

```bsl
"ВЫБРАТЬ ... ИЗ Документ.Заказ ГДЕ Ссылка В (&МассивСсылок)"
```

Instead of a loop `Для Каждого Ссылка`.

## Pitfalls

**Query in loop.** Classic N+1.
**LIKE '%text%'.** Index not used.
**Join without period filter.** Cartesian explosion.
**Mixed types in В().** Implicit casts.
**Heavy DISTINCT instead of key grouping.** Extra sort.
