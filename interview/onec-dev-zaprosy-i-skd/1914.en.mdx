# Optimizing dynamic list queries

## In short

The dynamic list runs its main query on open, scroll, and auto-refresh. Optimize with a short field list, strict filters on indexed parameters, no subqueries in SELECT, and heavy joins left to reports.

## How to answer in an interview

- I keep only column fields in the list, no extra joins.
- I set `&Период`, `&Организация` on form open.
- I remove computed fields with per-row queries.
- For search I use `СтрокаПоиска` on indexed attributes.
- I am honest: a list "like a report" with dozens of joins is unusable daily.

## How it works

Simplified list text:

```bsl
"ВЫБРАТЬ
|    Док.Ссылка,
|    Док.Дата,
|    Док.Номер,
|    Док.Контрагент
|ИЗ
|    Документ.РеализацияТоваров КАК Док
|ГДЕ
|    Док.Дата >= &НачалоПериода
|    И Док.Организация = &Организация"
```

Details load in the document card via a separate light query.

## Pitfalls

**Subquery in list field.** N+1 per visible row.
**No date filter.** Full document scan.
**Sort on non-indexed expression.** Filesort on large tables.
**RLS not in test.** Slow only for restricted roles.
**User filters without parameters.** Concatenation breaks plan cache.
