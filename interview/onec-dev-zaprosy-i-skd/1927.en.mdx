# Query temp table vs subquery in 1C

## In short

A subquery in WHERE or SELECT runs in the outer query context, often repeatedly. `ПОМЕСТИТЬ` materializes intermediate results once; good for heavy filters reused in several joins.

## How to answer in an interview

- I choose ПОМЕСТИТЬ when the subquery is reused in multiple joins or is expensive.
- Correlated subquery in SELECT per row becomes join or TT.
- A small independent subquery in WHERE may be cheaper than TT.
- I index TT in a follow-up query on large joins.
- I am honest: extra TT on a whole catalog without filter worsens the plan.

## How it works

```bsl
"ВЫБРАТЬ Ссылка ПОМЕСТИТЬ ВТКонтрагенты
|ИЗ Справочник.Контрагенты
|ГДЕ Регион = &Регион;
|ВЫБРАТЬ ...
|ИЗ ВТКонтрагенты КАК ВТ
|    ВНУТРЕННЕЕ СОЕДИНЕНИЕ Документ.Заказ КАК З
|    ПО ВТ.Ссылка = З.Контрагент"
```

## Pitfalls

**Correlated subquery in SELECT.** N executions.
**TT without filter.** Copy of large table in tempdb.
**Missing INDEX.** Slow next step.
**Duplicate TT and subquery.** Two strategies with different freshness.
**TT across unrelated batches.** Need temp table manager.
