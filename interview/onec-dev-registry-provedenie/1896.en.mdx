# Register totals freshness and rebuild

## In short

Totals on accumulation and periodic information registers speed up virtual tables. Freshness means aggregates match movements; on mismatch you need scheduled or manual totals rebuild.

## How to answer in an interview

- I watch for rebuild need after bulk load or backup restore.
- `.Остатки()` queries with stale totals can be slow or wrong.
- I schedule totals rebuild in maintenance windows on large bases.
- I disable totals on rarely read registers to save write cost.
- I am honest: rebuild on production during peak hours blocks users.

## How it works

Admin runs `ПересчитатьИтоги()` per register or globally. On posting the platform updates affected aggregates incrementally. Monitoring: compare balance from totals vs control query on movements sample.

## Pitfalls

**Ignoring stale totals.** Reports disagree with actual movements.
**Rebuild during business hours.** Long register locks.
**Totals off without reason.** Every report scans movements.
**Direct movement edit.** Totals diverge until rebuild.
**Mixing accumulation and information totals.** Different rebuild tools.
