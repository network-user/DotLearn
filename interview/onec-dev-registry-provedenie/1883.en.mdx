# Speeding up reads from a turnovers accumulation register

## In short

A turnovers-type register does not store balance totals; speed comes from proper dimensions, filters in the virtual table, DBMS indexes, and dropping unused fields. Aggregation goes to `.Обороты(,,)`.

## How to answer in an interview

- I narrow period and dimensions in `.Обороты(&Нач, &Кон, Склад = &Склад)`.
- I avoid joining the full register to catalogs without need.
- For heavy reports I use temp tables with pre-filtering.
- I split registers by domain instead of one universal register with many dimensions.
- I am honest: you cannot enable totals on a turnovers register; use a balance register for fast stock slices.

## How it works

```bsl
"ВЫБРАТЬ
|    Обор.Номенклатура,
|    Обор.КоличествоПриход,
|    Обор.КоличествоРасход
|ИЗ
|    РегистрНакопления.Продажи.Обороты(&Начало, &Конец, , ) КАК Обор
|ГДЕ
|    Обор.Склад = &Склад"
```

I cut the period to a month or shift, not full history. For scheduled reports I materialize intermediate results with `ПОМЕСТИТЬ`.

## Pitfalls

**Query over full history.** Full scan of the movements table.
**Extra joins.** Each join multiplies rows before aggregation.
**Confusion with balances.** Expecting a totals table on a turnovers register.
**Wrong register kind.** Balance task solved with the wrong metadata object.
**No recorder filter.** Extra rows in specialized reports.
