# Displacing calculation kinds when periods overlap

## In short

Displacement removes or reduces one accrual when another calculation kind is active in the same action period (leave displaces salary). The link is set in the chart of calculation types; the platform adjusts days and amounts in the calculation register.

## How to answer in an interview

- I separate displacement from base and leading kinds: here the conflict is over calendar days.
- Sick pay and leave typically displace salary per HR rules.
- On recalculation I review both displacing and displaced rows.
- Config without month-boundary tests gives double pay or gaps in days.
- I am honest: manual calculation register edits break automatic displacement.

## How it works

On leave posting the calculation register gets LeavePay; displacement shrinks salary action on overlapping days. The algorithm uses the work schedule and calendar in the configuration.

## Pitfalls

**Double pay for one day.** No displacement link between kinds.
**Wrong work schedule.** Displacement counts extra or missing days.
**No recalculation after leave change.** Salary not restored.
**Mixing with base kinds.** Different mechanisms; one setting does not replace the other.
**Manual register rows.** The platform does not recalculate displacement.
