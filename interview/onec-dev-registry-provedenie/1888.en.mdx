# Totals of a periodic information register

## In short

For a periodic information register the platform can maintain period totals: they speed up `.СрезПоследних()` and as-of-date slices. Totals rebuild on write and need refresh after bulk loads.

## How to answer in an interview

- I enable totals on heavy registers with millions of price or rate slices.
- `.СрезПоследних(&Дата)` uses totals when they are enabled and fresh.
- After exchange or load I check register totals freshness.
- Totals do not replace slice semantics: record period must be correct.
- I am honest: on small registers totals add little benefit and extra maintenance.

## How it works

Slice query:

```bsl
"РегистрСведений.КурсыВалют.СрезПоследних(&ДатаОтчета, Валюта = &Валюта)"
```

Administration: rebuild totals via processors or scheduled jobs after bulk write. A new rate on a date updates the affected totals range.

## Pitfalls

**Stale totals.** Report shows an outdated rate.
**Totals off, expecting speed.** Slice last scans all rows.
**Wrong register periodicity.** Slice behaves unlike business expects.
**Bulk load without rebuild.** Totals disagree with data.
**Confusion with accumulation register.** Different totals mechanics.
