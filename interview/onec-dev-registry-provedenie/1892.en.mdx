# What happens when document posting is canceled

## In short

Canceling posting clears the posted flag and deletes the document movements in subordinate registers inside a transaction. Balance register totals roll back; subordinate information registers clear by recorder.

## How to answer in an interview

- I call `Записать(РежимЗаписиДокумента.ОтменаПроведения)` or the Unpost command.
- Movements are removed by `Регистратор = Ссылка` filter, not manual SQL.
- After unpost the document can be edited and posted again.
- Related constraints (payment, period close) may block unpost.
- I am honest: unpost in a closed period is often blocked by accounting policy.

## How it works

```bsl
ДокументОбъект = ДокументСсылка.ПолучитьОбъект();
ДокументОбъект.Записать(РежимЗаписиДокумента.ОтменаПроведения);
```

The platform may additionally check in `ОбработкаУдаленияПроведения`. Calculation and accounting recorder movements are deleted too.

## Pitfalls

**Manual movement delete.** Document stays posted with empty registers.
**Unpost with dependent documents.** Base-document links block the operation.
**Ignoring ОбработкаУдаленияПроведения.** Business ban does not run.
**Partial unpost.** The platform does not standardly unpost one register only.
**Totals not updated.** Rare failure needs register totals rebuild.
