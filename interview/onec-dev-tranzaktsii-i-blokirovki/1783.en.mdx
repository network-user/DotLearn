# What locks occur when posting a document

## In short

On posting the platform opens a transaction, locks the recorder document, movements of related registers, and affected totals. The developer may add managed locks in the posting module to protect balances before writing movements.

## How to answer in an interview

- I separate platform automatic locks from explicit `БлокировкаДанных` in code.
- In an interview I list objects: document table, `_AccumReg*`, totals, sometimes number sequences.
- I stress that unposting removes movements in the same transactional model.
- I honestly say extra registers in movements multiply locks without benefit.
- I check `ОбработкаПроведения` and the list of `Движения.*.Записывать = Истина`.

## How it works

On `Записать(Проведение)` the platform runs posting handlers, builds movements, and writes them atomically.

```bsl
Процедура ОбработкаПроведения(Отказ, Режим)
    Блокировка = Новый БлокировкаДанных;
    Элемент = Блокировка.Добавить("РегистрНакопления.ТоварыНаСкладах");
    Элемент.Режим = РежимБлокировкиДанных.Исключительный;
    Элемент.УстановитьЗначение("Склад", Склад);
    Блокировка.Заблокировать();
    Движения.ТоварыНаСкладах.Записывать = Истина;
    Движение = Движения.ТоварыНаСкладах.Добавить();
    Движение.ВидДвижения = ВидДвиженияНакопления.Расход;
    Движение.Количество = КоличествоОтгрузки;
КонецПроцедуры
```

Register rows are locked automatically by dimension keys and recorder. On reposting old movements are deleted then new ones written; locks are held for the whole transaction.

Accumulation totals on backdated movement lock a range of aggregate periods. Periodic information registers lock period + dimensions slice.

If `Отказ = Истина`, the transaction rolls back and locks are released. On success commit frees resources on the DBMS side.

## Pitfalls

- **Lock after writing movements.** A parallel session may already change the balance.
- **Auto-registration of all document registers.** Extra tables in the lock graph.
- **Posting without managed lock on a hot register.** Races on write-off.
- **Reposting at peak load.** Double lock volume on the same keys.
- **Manual movements outside standard posting.** Locks do not match platform expectations.
